Beginners Batch

Correct Araimandi and Muzhumandi postures.
Detailed training in basic Tattu and Nattu Adavus.
Learning Asamyuta Hastas (Single hand gestures).
Basic introduction to Thalam (Rhythmic patterns).
Building discipline and physical stamina for dance.

Advanced Levels

Mastering fast-paced Jathis and complex footwork.
Introduction to Abhinaya (Expression) and Bhavam.
Learning the full Margam (traditional repertoire).
Samyuta Hastas (Double hand gestures) and Viniyogas.
Preparation for Grade examinations and public events.

Arangetram Coaching

Exclusive 1-on-1 intensive performance training.
Curating a full 2-hour solo performance repertoire.
Stage presence and makeup/costume guidance.
Practicing with a live professional Carnatic orchestra.

Adult Heritage

Focus on flexibility, poise, and graceful movements.
Stress relief through rhythmic and artistic movement.
Cultural appreciation through traditional storytelling.
Open to all levels, including total beginners.
